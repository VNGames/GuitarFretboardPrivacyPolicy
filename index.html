Privacy Policy

for Guitar Fretboard: Scales app

 

Guitar Fretboard: Scales app is designed to help you learn scales.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding policies with the collection and use of information by the app if anyone decided to use the app.

If you choose to use the app, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. Information collected are used for improving the app.

What information app collects?

App doesn't collect and use any Personal information. App has App Center integrated to collect crash reports, errors and analytics.

Here is App Center Privacy Policy

Crash reports and errors are used to improve the app, alongside information of the error App Center collects information about device manufacturer, android version, etc and that is used to investigate crash or error.

Analytics events are collecting information about use of app features like navigaion to a page in the app, toggling switch to enable/disable something, how long app session is, how many sessions per user per day. All of these analytics events are not collecting information on who they belong too. And are used to improve app by identifying what features are the most used and which are not used at all, etc.

All the settings in the app are stored locally and can be removed by uninstalling the app or cleaning app data.
